Welcome to Shrimp Disease Detection

This project aims to utilize machine learning and deep learning techniques to predict and diagnose diseases in shrimps. Shrimps are susceptible to various diseases that can significantly impact aquaculture production. Early detection and appropriate treatment are crucial for maintaining the health of shrimp populations and ensuring sustainable aquaculture practices.

Our platform provides a user-friendly interface for uploading shrimp images and obtaining predictions regarding potential diseases. By leveraging advanced algorithms and image processing techniques, we strive to enhance disease detection accuracy and facilitate prompt intervention measures.
